Results

For my activity I did a survey of people to see what the consensus was about in-home voice assistants.

My first question was: do you or would you own an in-home voice assistant (such as Google Home or Amazon Alexa)?

My next question was: do you think these devices are listening and recording what you say even before you say the activation word?

My next question was: do you know who has access to all your data that is being stored?

My last question was: is it ethical for these companies to always be recording with these devices?

I asked thirty three students at CSUEB these four questions.

20 people said they would or do own a Google Home.

18 people said they think the device is always listening.

22 people said they dont know who has access to the data these devices aquire.

23 people said they think it is unethical for these devices to always be listening.

It seems that people think the devices always lsitening is unethical but they would still own one. It is like a necessary evil that they are use to that comes witht he devices.
